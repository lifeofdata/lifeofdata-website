== Description ==

The [Coming Soon / Maintenance mode](http://readyshoppingcart.com/product/coming-soon-plugin-pro-version/ "WordPress Coming Soon Plugin") Ready! plugin is the best solution for your website, while it's under construction.

Professional and simple way to setup launch page, the Coming Soon / Maintenance mode Ready! works with any theme on any hosting.

More over you can use it as:

* Coming Soon Page (pre-set templates and custom styles available)
* Maintenance Mode / Redirect 301
* UnderConstruction HTTP 503 - Service Unavailable

**Features**

* Works with any WordPress Theme
* Build with HTML5 & CSS3
* Responsive. Work on any device
* Access controls (Restrict By User Role, IP Address,)
* Social integration (Facebook, Tweeter, Google+, YouTube, Instagram)
* Custom Favicon support
* Multisite Support
* BuddyPress Support
* Google Anaylics integration
* Free Coming soon page templates!
* Easy and user-friendly setup
* 3 Coming soon modes: Coming soon page, Redirect and show 503 HTTP
* Subscribe feature
* Flexible and easy coming soon page template setup
* SEO (Search Engine Optimization) options and Meta Inforamtion available

[youtube http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kIRh4oCvv6I]

The Coming Soon/Maintenance Mode Ready! plugin is what you can and should use to notify your customers that your website is currently under construction and is about to be launched. Thanks to high theme compatibility, you can style your Coming Soon page using a variety of preset templates and custom style options. This is one of the few existing plugins allowing you to make your website attractive before actually launching it. By styling your Coming Soon page, you make your potential clients want to check your website every day and look forward to the launch day!

Coming Soon/Maintenance Mode Ready works with any WordPress theme and on any device. If you have installed this plugin, you can set it up to show any of the three messages: Coming Soon, 503 HTTP and Redirect. Setup is easy and effortless. You can watch a video tutorial and follow the steps highlighted in it.

The plugin combines a variety of advanced features, including, but not limited to Google Anaylics integration, SEO and Meta Information options, social integration (Tweeter, YouTube, Google+, Instagram, etc.), BuddyPress support, user email subscription options.
